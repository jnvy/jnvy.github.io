    Commission Rules, Mar 22, 2019

    I take Payment after sketching! I will not finish the illustration until payment is made in full.

    Commissions are for non commercial use only* Unless discussed during the commission process! Please know that my commercial type commissions are higher in price, if you're interested please email me or DM me via twitter with all of the info and an offer.

    *Meaning you CANNOT use your non commercial commission for profit. Example; T-shirts, prints, buttons, etc. You CAN use my art for: icons and display pictures on social media, Youtube thumbnails or headers, graphics for your stream, business cards* etc.

    Please do not repost my art without credit, and/or claim it as your own, do not edit my work unless given permission.
