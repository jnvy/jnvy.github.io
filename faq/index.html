TERMS OF SERVICE

Commission Service Terms, Mar 22, 2019

Prices are subject to change depanding on demand.

I have the right to turn a commission down.

I have the right to modify commissions for advertisement purposes.

Please do not sell, steal, trace, or heavily reference my art without credit, and/or claim it as your own.

Previous commissions art style may not be 100% replicated due to changes in art style.

Commissions are to only be used by the commissioner, unless stated otherwise.

Commissions mute be used for their exact purposes (emotes must be used for emotes).

Refunds and cancelling the commissions are not allowed AFTER the sketching phase.

PAYMENT INFORMATION

Commission Payments, Mar 22, 2019

Payment is done upfront in full after the sketching phase is completed through PAYPAL

If you are located outside of the United States, you are liable for included fees.
